# Citadel Dispatch Module Catalog

Each issue should include only modules justified by real signal.

## Core modules

- **Lead Signal**: the most consequential development and why it leads.
- **Protocol & Policy**: consensus/policy debates, mempool policy, relay norms.
- **Implementation Watch**: material changes in major clients/repos.
- **Network Signals**: observed network behavior shifts worth attention.
- **Points of Contention**: serious disagreements, framed fairly.
- **Why It Matters**: second-order implications and affected stakeholders.
- **Primary Sources**: direct links with one-line reason per link.
- **Watchlist**: unresolved items likely to matter soon.

## Optional specialist modules

- **Context**: historical or architectural backdrop for current developments.
- **Data Snapshot**: compact metrics block with methods note.
- **Release Radar**: upcoming releases and risk/relevance note.
- **BIP Watch**: movement in BIP lifecycle or notable discussion.
- **Fork Watch**: activation/fork coordination status and open risks.
- **Governance Notes**: process legitimacy, representation, and coordination issues.
- **Open Questions**: clearly unresolved technical or social questions.

## Module quality checks

Every included module should answer:

1. What changed?
2. Who is affected?
3. What evidence supports this?
4. What remains uncertain?
5. Where can readers verify directly?
