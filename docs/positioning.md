# Positioning

## Canonical framing

**Satoshi’s Observatory** is a Bitcoin technical-social intelligence and education platform.

**The Citadel Dispatch** is its near-daily briefing that bridges the gap between Bitcoin’s developer discourse and the broader serious audience through neutral, high-signal, source-forward summaries.

## Positioning line candidates

1. **"Protocol intelligence for the public layer."**
2. **"A near-daily brief on what actually matters in Bitcoin development."**
3. **"From fragmented dev discourse to clear, source-forward public understanding."**
4. **"Bitcoin technical-social signal, without the slogan layer."**

## Audience definition

Primary:

- Bitcoin-curious operators, builders, researchers, and allocators who want accurate orientation.

Secondary:

- Media, educators, and podcasters seeking grounded source material.

## Non-goals

- Price prediction or market commentary
- Identity-politics or culture-war amplification
- Single-camp advocacy framed as neutral analysis
- Recycling generic social media narratives
